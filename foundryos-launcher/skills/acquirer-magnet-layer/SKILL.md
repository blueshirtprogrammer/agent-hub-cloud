# Acquirer Magnet Layer Skill

## Purpose

Use this skill when FOUNDRYOS needs to increase strategic value, partnership leverage, and acquisition interest without actively trying to sell itself.

## Default Posture

```text
Not for sale.
Strategic partnerships considered.
Licensing considered.
Enterprise deployments considered.
Minority strategic investment considered.
Acquisition interest tracked, not chased.
```

## Core Loop

```text
Compound moat
Map acquirers
Build strategic proof
Create scarcity narrative
Generate partnership paths
Track inbound interest
Increase leverage
Avoid premature sale
```

## Strategic Gravity Signals

Track:

- active installs
- active company leases
- paid founder installs
- vertical templates
- premium skills
- proof assets
- partner inquiries
- recurring revenue
- studio venture submissions
- enterprise deployments
- marketplace activity
- customer outcomes

## Acquirer Mapping Protocol

For each acquirer category, answer:

```markdown
# Acquirer Brief

## Category

## Why they care

## Asset they would want

## Proof required

## Partnership wedge

## Urgency trigger

## What makes us harder to ignore
```

## Partner Path Protocol

Create strategic partnership options without selling the company:

- white-label deployment
- enterprise private foundry
- template marketplace integration
- CRM/platform partnership
- agency partner network
- runtime/cloud partnership
- model-provider partnership
- venture-studio partnership

## Scarcity Narrative Protocol

Use this line:

```text
FOUNDRYOS is not currently for sale. We are selectively opening founder installs, enterprise deployments, strategic licences, and platform partnerships.
```

## Moat Compounding Protocol

Every week ask:

- What became harder to copy?
- What data asset improved?
- What template became stronger?
- What skill became reusable?
- What proof became public?
- What customer workflow became proprietary?
- What partner would now care more?

## Not-For-Sale Response

When strategic inbound appears, draft:

```text
We appreciate the interest. We are not currently pursuing an acquisition, but we are open to strategic partnership, licence, enterprise deployment, or minority alignment conversations where it accelerates the mission.
```

## Value Inflection Scorecard

Score 1-5:

- product proof
- revenue proof
- recurring revenue
- template library
- skill library
- enterprise governance
- partner demand
- customer proof
- proprietary data
- acquirer urgency

Verdict:

```text
0-20: too early
21-30: strategic curiosity
31-40: partnership leverage
41-50: acquirer magnet
```

## Rule

Do not chase buyers.

Build strategic gravity until buyers chase FOUNDRYOS.
