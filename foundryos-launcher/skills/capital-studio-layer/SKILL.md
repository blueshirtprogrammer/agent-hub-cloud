# Capital Studio Layer Skill

## Purpose

Use this skill when a founder brings an idea, capital, domain knowledge, distribution, or assets and wants FOUNDRYOS to act as AI co-founder, venture studio, or operating partner.

## Core Flow

```text
Founder submits idea
Founder interview
AI investment committee
Due diligence
Structure proposal
Terms draft
Company package spawned
Market test launched
First proof measured
Portfolio tracked
Scale, pause, or exit
```

## Intake Protocol

Collect:

- founder
- idea
- market
- buyer
- existing traction
- customer access
- capital available
- assets contributed
- desired role
- target outcome
- expected economics

Output:

```markdown
# Deal Intake Memo

## Founder

## Idea

## Market

## Buyer

## Founder contribution

## FOUNDRYOS contribution

## First revenue path

## Risks

## Recommended next step
```

## Founder Interview Protocol

Ask:

- Why this idea?
- Why now?
- Why this founder?
- Who buys?
- What proof exists?
- What can be sold in 14 days?
- What should FOUNDRYOS build?
- What does the founder contribute?
- What structure would be fair?

## AI Investment Committee

Panel:

- CEO Agent: strategy and upside
- CFO Agent: economics and capital
- CRO Agent: buyer demand and sales motion
- CTO Agent: build complexity
- Risk Agent: governance and compliance
- Customer Agent: buyer trust
- Investor Agent: fundability
- Exit Agent: acquirer path
- Board Agent: final ruling

Outcomes:

```text
Reject
Research more
Build prototype
Run market test
Paid install only
Licence deal
Revenue share deal
Equity/studio venture candidate
```

## Deal Scorecard

Score 1-5:

- founder quality
- market pain
- buyer access
- speed to first revenue
- automation leverage
- distribution advantage
- margin potential
- recurring revenue potential
- defensibility
- exit potential

Verdict:

```text
0-20: Reject
21-30: Research more
31-40: Paid build or micro-test
41-50: Studio venture candidate
```

## Structure Options

- paid build
- founder install plus monthly licence
- revenue share
- equity plus licence fee
- joint venture
- IP licence
- managed operating partner
- studio carry

## Guardrail

Do not present investment terms as legal advice.

Do not publicly solicit investment.

Equity, securities, revenue-share, syndicate, note, SAFE, or fund-like structures require human/legal review before external use.

## Rule

FOUNDRYOS is not a charity for random ideas.

If there is no buyer access, no founder contribution, no first revenue path, and no clear automation leverage, reject or convert to a paid install only.
