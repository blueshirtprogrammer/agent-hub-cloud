# FOUNDRYOS Capital Studio Layer

## Purpose

FOUNDRYOS becomes more than a venture launcher.

It becomes an AI-native venture studio and capital formation layer where founders can bring ideas, contribute domain knowledge, invest capital, and have FOUNDRYOS act as an AI co-founder system that builds, tests, launches, sells, and scales the company.

## Core concept

```text
Founder brings idea, access, insight, or capital
FOUNDRYOS runs investment committee
FOUNDRYOS stress-tests the venture
FOUNDRYOS builds the company package
FOUNDRYOS launches the first market test
FOUNDRYOS executes revenue motion
Founder contributes as domain owner or investor
FOUNDRYOS earns equity, revenue share, licence fees, or studio carry
```

## Founder Interview Agent

Interviews the founder like a VC partner and operating co-founder.

Questions:

- Why this idea?
- Why now?
- Why you?
- Who buys?
- What proof exists?
- What unfair advantage exists?
- What can be sold in 14 days?
- What can FOUNDRYOS automate or build?
- What does the founder contribute?
- What economics are fair?

## Capital modes

### Founder-funded

Founder pays setup and monthly lease.

### Studio-backed

FOUNDRYOS contributes build/ops in exchange for equity or revenue share.

### Hybrid

Founder pays reduced cash plus equity/rev-share.

### Investor-backed

Founder or external capital funds the venture, and FOUNDRYOS receives platform, licence, or studio economics.

## Investor compliance guardrail

This layer should not publicly solicit investments or create securities offerings without proper legal review.

All investment, equity, revenue share, SAFE, note, syndicate, or fund-like structures require human/legal review before external use.

## Deal scorecard

Score 1-5:

- founder quality
- market pain
- buyer access
- speed to first revenue
- automation leverage
- distribution advantage
- margin potential
- recurring revenue potential
- defensibility
- exit potential

Verdict:

```text
0-20: Reject
21-30: Research more
31-40: Paid build or micro-test
41-50: Studio venture candidate
```

## Rule

FOUNDRYOS should not accept every idea.

It should behave like a ruthless AI venture partner.

If the founder has no insight, no buyer access, no capital, no useful contribution, and no credible first revenue path, the deal should be rejected or converted to a paid install only.
