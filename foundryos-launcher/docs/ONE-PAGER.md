# FOUNDRYOS™ Launcher One-Pager

## Headline

**Deploy AI companies, not AI assistants.**

## What it is

FOUNDRYOS Launcher is a one-click deployment platform for AI-run company packages.

A user describes the company they want, selects a template, connects approved tools, imports the company into Paperclip, installs skills, and deploys the operating layer locally or to cloud.

## Why it matters

AI tools are powerful but fragmented.

Founders and operators are stuck stitching together:

- coding agents,
- browser agents,
- model routers,
- CRMs,
- billing systems,
- email tools,
- deployment platforms,
- docs,
- and strategy prompts.

FOUNDRYOS packages these into deployable companies with org charts, departments, revenue loops, approval gates, and proof-of-work.
