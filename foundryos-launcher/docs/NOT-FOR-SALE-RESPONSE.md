# FOUNDRYOS Not-For-Sale Response

## Purpose

Use this when strategic inbound appears from a potential acquirer, investor, platform, enterprise, agency network, or venture studio.

## Default posture

```text
FOUNDRYOS is not currently for sale.
We are selectively exploring strategic partnerships, licensing, enterprise deployments, platform integrations, and minority strategic alignment where it accelerates the mission.
```

## Short response

```text
Appreciate the interest. We are not currently pursuing an acquisition, but we are open to strategic partnership, licensing, enterprise deployment, or minority alignment conversations where it clearly accelerates the FOUNDRYOS mission.
```

## Strategic partnership response

```text
We are building FOUNDRYOS as the operating layer for deployable AI companies.

Rather than discussing acquisition, the better starting point may be a strategic deployment, platform integration, licence arrangement, or private foundry pilot.

If there is a clear mutual advantage, we can explore a structured partnership conversation.
```

## Enterprise response

```text
FOUNDRYOS is not positioned as a tool purchase. It is a deployable AI company operating layer.

For enterprise groups, the right model is usually a private foundry deployment, governed agent company, or licensed vertical company template.
```

## Investor response

```text
We are not raising broadly at this stage.

We may consider strategic capital where the investor brings distribution, platform leverage, enterprise access, infrastructure advantage, or material acceleration beyond cash.
```

## Discovery questions

- What specifically made FOUNDRYOS strategically interesting?
- Which asset is most valuable to you: templates, skills, marketplace, runtime, governance, revenue autopilot, or venture studio layer?
- Are you seeking partnership, licence, integration, investment, or acquisition?
- What distribution, infrastructure, or customer access can you bring?
- What would a successful 30-day strategic pilot look like?

## Leverage rule

Do not reveal sensitive data, customer lists, source code, private economics, or internal roadmap until NDA/commercial process is approved.

## Next step options

```text
Partnership discovery call
Enterprise private foundry pilot
Platform integration memo
Strategic licence proposal
Minority strategic alignment review
No fit / decline
```

## Internal action

Any serious inbound should trigger:

- acquirer brief
- strategic fit memo
- risk review
- partnership option map
- board agent ruling
- owner summary
