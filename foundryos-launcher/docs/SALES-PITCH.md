We think that is too small.

FOUNDRYOS packages those into deployable company templates.

So instead of saying, “Here is a chatbot that helps with tasks,” we say:

> “Here is an AI company that can be installed, configured, and pointed at a market.”

The same engine can produce other niche company templates: property management growth, AI agency ops, unattended retail media, or enterprise internal AI teams.

## Objection handling

### “Is this just agents?”

No. Agents are workers. FOUNDRYOS is the company structure, deployment layer, skill system, revenue loop, and governance wrapper around them.

### “Can it run fully autonomously?”

For internal low-risk work, yes over time. For external commercial/legal/financial actions, it stays approval-gated. That is what makes it enterprise-usable.

### “Can I use my existing tools?”

Yes. The point is to route work through approved harnesses: GitHub, Paperclip, OpenRouter, Codex, OpenCode, Kilo, CRM, billing, email, deployment, and docs.

### “What does it replace?”

It does not replace the founder. It replaces the chaos between idea, execution, sales, delivery, reporting, and scale.

### “What do I get first?”

A deployable company shell, first niche template, agent org chart, skill pack, and a revenue sprint that can start producing commercial action.
