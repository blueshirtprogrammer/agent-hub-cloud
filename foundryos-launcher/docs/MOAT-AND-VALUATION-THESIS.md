# FOUNDRYOS Moat and Valuation Thesis

## Category moat

FOUNDRYOS is not another assistant layer.

It is a company deployment layer.

The moat forms when the system compounds across:

- templates
- skills
- proof
- customer workflows
- distribution channels
- licensing
- governance
- venture intake
- portfolio intelligence

## Asset compounding

### Template library

Every company package becomes reusable.

### Skill library

Every repeated workflow becomes a skill.

### Operating history

Every task, proposal, case study, install, and objection improves the system.

### Licence heartbeat

Recurring access becomes enforceable and measurable.

### Distribution surfaces

Every marketplace/plugin/package increases discoverability.

### Capital studio

Every founder submission improves venture pattern recognition.

### Acquirer magnet

Every proof asset increases strategic gravity.

## Pre-seed narrative

The company should be judged on speed to proof, quality of category design, first paid installs, template repeatability, and channel surface area.

## Series A narrative

The company should be judged on recurring revenue, enterprise deployments, marketplace liquidity, template performance, retention, and distribution leverage.

## Not-for-sale posture

Acquisition interest should be tracked but not chased.

The stronger path is to grow strategic dependency through licences, enterprise deployments, integrations, and partner distribution.
