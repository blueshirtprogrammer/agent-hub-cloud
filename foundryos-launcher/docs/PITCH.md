# FOUNDRYOS™ Launcher Pitch

## One-liner

FOUNDRYOS Launcher is a one-click deployment platform for AI-run companies: describe the company, choose the template, connect tools, deploy the agent org chart, and start revenue execution.

## Short pitch

Most AI agent products give businesses a better employee.

FOUNDRYOS gives founders and operators something much more valuable:

> **A deployable company.**

A user can stand up an AI-run venture with a CEO, C-suite, operating departments, revenue agents, proposal and invoice workflows, proof-of-work reporting, and capital/exit readiness.

The human is no longer the operator. The human becomes the investor, board chair, and approval authority.

## The problem

AI tools are fragmented.

Founders are drowning in:

- disconnected coding agents,
- browser agents,
- model routers,
- CRMs,
- billing tools,
- email tools,
- automation platforms,
- pitch docs,
- investor assets,
- and operating playbooks.

Most people can generate plans. Very few can turn those tools into a working company that finds customers, sells, invoices, delivers, reports, and improves.

## The insight

The killer application of agents is not replacing a single job.

It is packaging an entire operating company into an installable system.

That means:

- company templates,
- AI org charts,
- skills,
- harnesses,
- workflows,
- revenue loops,
- approval gates,
- and deployment infrastructure.

## The product

FOUNDRYOS Launcher provides:

1. Company template picker.
2. Credential connector cockpit.
3. Paperclip company import.
4. Agent skill installation.
5. Harness routing for IDE/business execution tools.
6. Deployment to local, Vercel, Fly.io, or self-hosted infrastructure.
7. Marketplace for paid company templates.
8. Agent-hour and company-lease monetisation.

Hospitality has high-value operational problems that are easy to demonstrate:

- guest revenue leakage,
- slow procurement,

- labour pressure,
- under-monetised rooms and venues,

- poor reporting loops.

A launcher that can deploy a full AI company around that problem is more credible than a generic agent playground.
