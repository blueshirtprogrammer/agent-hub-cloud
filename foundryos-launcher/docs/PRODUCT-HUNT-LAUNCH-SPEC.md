## FAQ

### Is this another AI agent tool?

No. Agents are workers. FOUNDRYOS packages the company structure, approval chain, revenue loop, licence system, and deployment layer around them.

### Who is it for?

AI agencies, automation consultants, venture studios, enterprise innovation teams, and founders who want to deploy niche company shells quickly.

### Can it run fully autonomously?

It can run internal strategy, drafting, planning, scoring, and execution prep autonomously. External, legal, financial, or customer-impacting actions route through executive approval chains.

### What can I buy first?

Founder installs, company leases, skill/template packs, and enterprise private foundry setups.
