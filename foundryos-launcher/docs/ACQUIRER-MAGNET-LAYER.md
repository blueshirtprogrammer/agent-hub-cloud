# FOUNDRYOS Acquirer Magnet Layer

## Purpose

FOUNDRYOS should become strategically valuable enough that acquirers, platforms, agencies, venture studios, and enterprise buyers want access before the company is formally for sale.

The posture is not desperate selling.

The posture is:

```text
Not for sale.
Strategic partnerships considered.
Licensing considered.
Enterprise deployments considered.
Minority strategic investment considered.
Acquisition interest tracked, not chased.
```

## Core thesis

The strongest companies are not sold because they beg.

They are pursued because they compound assets others cannot easily recreate.

FOUNDRYOS compounds:

- installable AI company templates
- operating history
- skill libraries
- customer proof
- licence revenue
- vertical venture packages
- agent governance workflows
- founder/deal intake data
- portfolio intelligence
- reusable GTM systems
- proof-of-work archives
- marketplace leverage
